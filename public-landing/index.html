The Great Hunt

Resale research for treasure hunters.

The Great Hunt is a prototype app for people evaluating estate sale, auction, antique shop, marketplace, and retail closeout finds.

The app uses marketplace data to help users research comparable prices, review active and sold comps, and check spread, condition, and friction before they spend money.

The Great Hunt does not allow users to sign in with eBay, does not store eBay account data, and does not manage eBay listings, orders, seller activity, messages, or payments.

Status: prototype / pre-launch
